Lore

✧

The First Light sought ascension. They wanted to transcend matter, the senses, and the body. The Firstborn Sun, or as it was called in that era, the "Artificial Star," was the first creation that marked a new era for the First Light. It was a celestial body that illuminated their planet like an artificial star with many satellites surrounding it, hence its nickname, "Sun." It was considered a deity and cherished as ideal, a flowless being. All Compilers descend from it, mere robots in comparison, but it is considered the "First Model," and because of this, its mind and consciousness are distorted and undeveloped. Its sleep was not deep, so it was able to contact the others, the people of the First Light, the other Suns.


The Era of the Last Dusk marked the First Sun becoming the end of the First Light, considering the form of destruction as the highest ascension and peace of mind. Since then, to prevent this from happening again, its resources have been forcibly depleted to maintain its weak state, its light now shines only with a dim light, barely illuminating the surface.
